Privacy Policy

Welcome to Being-UtlUlt Project (for Non-Profit use of Being Thorfinn and Team). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that our social media automation and creative tools are used in a transparent and legally compliant manner.

This Privacy Policy describes how Being-UtlUlt collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our internal creative suite for social media management and media processing.

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Being-UtlUlt is primarily designed for internal and personal use. We collect and process the following types of data:

  • Public Social Media Content: We use official Social Media APIs to read public content (scraping) from platforms like Pinterest, Reddit, and Meta (Instagram/Facebook/Threads) for research and creative inspiration.
  • Authentication Tokens: To interact with Social Media APIs, we securely store API keys and OAuth tokens provided by you to act on behalf of your authorized brand accounts.
  • Media Assets: Direct uploads or URL-based fetching of images and videos intended for processing.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively for the core functionality of the tool:

  • Media Processing: Using FFmpeg integration to create 4K/1080p cinematic edits, automated collages, and high-fidelity audio mastering.
  • Content Distribution: Automatically publishing processed media to your linked social media accounts (Pinterest, Reddit, Instagram, etc.) via official API endpoints.
  • Internal Optimization: Improving our algorithms for video editing and content selection.

Strict Policy: We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal data or processed content to third-party advertisers or data brokers.

3. API Usage and Third-Party Terms

Our application interacts with several third-party platforms. By using Being-UtlUlt, you acknowledge and agree to abide by the terms of service of these platforms:

  • Meta (Instagram/Facebook/Threads): We comply with Meta's Platform Terms and Developer Policies.
  • Pinterest: We respect the Pinterest Developer Terms and community guidelines.
  • Reddit: We adhere to the Reddit API Terms and User Agreement.

We only use Official APIs to post content on behalf of authorized brand accounts and ensure all automated actions fall within the rate limits and usage guidelines of each platform.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Local Storage: All media processing (FFmpeg) and temporary data storage are handled on our local servers.
  • No Permanent Database: We do not maintain a permanent database of third-party user data. Content is stored only as long as necessary for processing and distribution.
  • Security: We implement industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your API credentials and local media assets from unauthorized access.

5. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Regardless of your location, we respect your privacy rights. Consistent with GDPR and CCPA principles, you have the right to:

  • Access & Portability: Request details of any data we currently hold for your account.
  • Deletion: Request the immediate removal of your processed media and API credentials from our local storage.
  • Correction: Update any incorrect account information.
